**The Integrity Protocol — Week 1**
*The Moment You Stop Pretending*

---

**If You're Here, It's Probably Because Something Broke.**

Maybe she found something.

Maybe you finally told her.

Maybe the look on her face is still with you — and you don't know how to un-see it.

You're not here because you woke up one day and decided to do some self-improvement.

You're here because something happened. Something real. And someone you love is living with the fallout right now.

That's not nothing.

That's actually the most important thing you could bring into this program.

---

**This Isn't Just About You Anymore.**

You've probably spent a long time keeping this hidden.

Managing it. Minimizing it. Telling yourself it wasn't that serious.

But the moment she found out — or the moment you finally said it out loud — the secret stopped being yours alone.

Now there's two of you carrying this.

And she didn't sign up for it.

This program won't fix your relationship. That's not what this is.

But what you do in the next 16 weeks will determine whether repair is even possible.

That's the weight of this.

You should feel it.

---

**You Are Not Weak. But You Have Work to Do.**

Your brain built these patterns over years — sometimes decades.

Reinforcement loops. Escape routes. Rituals you ran to when life got hard, lonely, or numb.

That's not an excuse.

It's a map.

And maps can be redrawn.

But only if you're willing to stop pretending the old one was working.

---

**Before You Go Further — One Honest Moment**

*You don't have to have it all figured out to start.*

You just have to be willing to be honest — maybe for the first time in a long time.

With yourself.

And eventually, with her.

That starts here. This week.

---

**Week 1 Commitment Statement**

*I am here because something needs to change. Not just for me — for the people I've hurt by staying hidden. I am willing to be honest, even when it's hard. That starts today.*
